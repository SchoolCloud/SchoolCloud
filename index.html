STUDENT FINDER
is the feature that finds your student. With a simple search, it is able to advise what period, classroom, and teacher your student is currently located in to insure a faster pick-up time.

ATTENDANCE
is a future feature that will enable not only teachers and administrators, but also parents and students, to track your student's attendance in all of their classes.

GRADEBOOK
is a future feature that will allow teachers to easily input and keep track of all students' grades. This feature will also allow parents and students to visually see their student's progress in their classes.
