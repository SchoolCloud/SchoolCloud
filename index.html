About

Creative and Energy Driven

Social Profiles

Created by Teachers, for Teachers

Founded in 2017, SchoolCloud is the brain child of two San Antonio teachers who were tired of compromising their productivity through the inefficient Learning Management Systems given to them by their schools. By creating an intuitive, responsive, and secure application for teachers, they reduced the amount of interruptions and time spent recovering lost data for themselves and their fellow teachers.

- Joel Gilbert taught fifth grade math at a local charter school until SchoolCloud took off. Over three years, he had a 100% average pass rate for the STAAR exams, for over 140 fifth grade students. He now free lances while maintaining and building more SchoolCloud features!

- Andrew Krummenacher currently teaches technology at a local San Antonio charter school where where students use coding, 3-D printers, and their own creativity to develop their own creations!

- Curt Arbtin is a local parent who works professionally developing code for the US government.
